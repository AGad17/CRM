The Challenge

We were flying blind

Managing accounts across Egypt, KSA, UAE, Bahrain & Jordan — with no single source of truth.

📋

Scattered Data

Revenue, contracts, and churn tracked across multiple spreadsheets with no consistency.

🔍

No Visibility

Couldn't answer basic questions: What's our MRR? Which accounts are at risk? Who owns what?

⚡

Manual Everything

Invoices, pipeline updates, and churn tracking done by hand — slow, error-prone, and painful.

The Solution

One platform. Complete clarity.

ShopBrain CRM brings every part of our revenue operation into a single, real-time, role-aware platform.

Real-time

Data syncs automatically. MRR, pipeline, and onboarding status always up to date.

Role-aware

Every team member sees exactly what they need — no more, no less.

End-to-end

From first sales touch to invoicing to churn — the entire customer lifecycle in one place.

Access Control

The Right Access for Every Role

Custom roles with granular per-module permissions. Admins assign roles — no re-login needed.

👑

CCO Admin

Full access to all modules, settings, and user management. Can create custom roles and assign them.

💼

Revenue Manager

Write access to pipeline and invoicing. Can create deals, update stages, manage invoices.

🤝

Customer Success

Operations focus — manage customer journey phases, CSAT/NPS, and account notes.

🔧 Admins can create custom roles with any combination of module permissions — view, create, edit, or delete — per team member.
